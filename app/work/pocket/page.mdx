# Pocket

**The web home of Pocket, Mozilla's article-discovery service. A multi-surface editorial and personal-library platform that served more than 2 million daily page views at its peak. I was hired in 2017 to work on the first rewrite of the web application with one other engineer, Joel Kelly; in 2020 I co-architected the second rewrite. I stayed through the Mozilla acquisition and up to the product's eventual shutdown, six years total.**
