# Crowd

**A location-based, ephemeral messaging platform where your identity exists only as long as your content does. Messages are visible only within a radius, and only for a limited time. When everything you've posted expires, your identity expires with it.**

In active development · [Source](https://github.com/anthony-liddle/crowd)
